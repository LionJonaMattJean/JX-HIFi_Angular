Shipping Policy

Most products will ship from our facility within 1-2 business days after order placement. For products over 150 lbs, please refer to our freight page for more details.

Express shipment orders placed after 12PM EST will be shipped the next business day. Our free shipping option (orders over $49 to contiguous US) will ship via the cheapest ground service.
